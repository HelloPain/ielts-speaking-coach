# IELTS Speaking Part 1 Practice Question Bank

Use this bank as the default source for Part 1 teaching and mock-exam questions. These are user-supplied practice questions, not verified predictions or guaranteed current exam questions.

## Contents

- Core topics: Work/Study; Home/Accommodation; Hometown; The Area You Live In
- Additional topics: Plants; Public Places; Rules; Shoes; Crowded Places; Going Out; Spending Time with Older People; Growing Vegetables and Fruit; Chatting; Borrowing and Lending; Advertisements; Museums; Having a Break; Sharing; Doing Something Well; Food; Pets and Animals; Sports Teams

## Core topics

### Work/Study

- Are you a student or a worker?
- What work do you do?
- Why did you choose that type of work?
- Do you like your job?
- What requirements did you need to meet to get your current job?
- Do you have any plans for your work in the next five years?
- What do you think is most important at the moment?
- Do you want to change to another job?
- What technology do you use at work?
- Who helps you the most, and how?
- What are the benefits of being your age?

### Home/Accommodation

- What kind of house or apartment would you like to live in in the future?
- Are the transport facilities near your home good?
- Do you prefer living in a house or an apartment?
- Please describe the room you live in.
- What part of your home do you like the most?
- How long have you lived there?
- Do you plan to live there for a long time?
- What's the difference between where you live now and places where you have lived in the past?
- Can you describe the place where you live?
- Which room does your family spend the most time in?
- What's your favourite room in your apartment or house?
- What makes you feel comfortable in your home?
- Do you think it is important to live in a comfortable environment?
- Do you live in an apartment or a house?
- What do you usually do at home?
- What kind of accommodation do you live in?

### Hometown

- Where is your hometown?
- Is it a big city or a small place?
- Please describe your hometown a little.
- How long have you lived there?
- Do you think you will continue living there for a long time?
- Do you like your hometown?
- Do you like living there?
- What do you like most about your hometown?
- Is there anything you dislike about it?
- What's your hometown famous for?
- Did you learn about the history of your hometown at school?
- Are there many young people in your hometown?
- Is your hometown a good place for young people to pursue their careers?
- Have you learned anything about the history of your hometown?
- Did you learn about the culture of your hometown when you were a child?

### The Area You Live In

- Do you like the area where you live?
- Where do you like to go in that area?
- Do you know any famous people in your area?
- What changes have there been in the area recently?
- Do you know any of your neighbours?
- Are the people in your neighbourhood nice and friendly?
- Do you live in a noisy or quiet area?

## Additional topics

### Plants

- Do you keep plants at home?
- What plants did you grow when you were young?
- Do you know anything about growing plants?
- Do people in China send plants as gifts?

### Public Places

- Have you ever talked with someone you didn't know in a public place?
- Do you wear headphones in public places?
- Would you like to see more public places near where you live?
- Do you often go to public places with your friends?

### Rules

- Are there any rules for students at your school?
- Do you think students would benefit from having more rules?
- Do you prefer to have more or fewer rules at school?
- Have you ever had a really dedicated teacher?
- Have you ever had a really strict teacher?
- Would you like to work as a teacher in a rule-free school?

### Shoes

- Do you like buying shoes? How often do you buy them?
- Have you ever bought shoes online?
- How much money do you usually spend on shoes?
- Which do you prefer, fashionable shoes or comfortable shoes?

### Crowded Places

- Is the city where you live crowded?
- Is there a crowded place near where you live?
- Do you like crowded places?
- Do most people like crowded places?
- When was the last time you were in a crowded place?

### Going Out

- Do you bring food or snacks with you when you go out?
- Do you always take your mobile phone with you when you go out?
- Do you often carry cash with you?
- How often do you use cash?

### Spending Time with Older People

- Have you ever worked with older people?
- Would you be happy to work with people who are older than you?
- What are the benefits of being friends with or working with older people?
- Do you enjoy spending time with older people?

### Growing Vegetables and Fruit

- Are you interested in growing vegetables and fruit?
- Is growing vegetables popular in your country?
- Do many people grow vegetables in your city?
- Do you think it's easy to grow vegetables?
- Should schools teach students how to grow vegetables?

### Chatting

- Do you like chatting with friends?
- What do you usually chat about with friends?
- Do you prefer chatting with a group of people or with just one friend?
- Do you prefer to communicate face to face or via social media?
- Do you ever argue with your friends?

### Borrowing and Lending

- Have you ever borrowed books from other people?
- Have you ever borrowed money from someone?
- Do you like lending things to other people?
- How do you feel when people don't return things they borrowed from you?
- Do you mind if other people borrow money from you?

### Advertisements

- Is there an advertisement that made an impression on you when you were a child?
- Do you see a lot of advertisements on trains or other forms of transport?
- Do you like advertisements?
- What kind of advertising do you like?
- Do you often see advertisements when you use your phone or computer?

### Museums

- Do you think museums are important?
- Are there many museums in your hometown?
- Do you often visit museums?
- When was the last time you visited a museum?

### Having a Break

- How often do you take a rest or a break?
- What do you usually do when you are resting?
- Do you take a nap when you have a break?
- How do you feel after taking a nap?

### Sharing

- Did your parents teach you to share when you were a child?
- What kind of things do you like to share with others?
- What kind of things are not suitable for sharing?
- Have you shared anything with other people recently?
- Who is the first person you would like to share good news with?
- Do you prefer to share news with your friends or your parents?

### Doing Something Well

- Can you describe a time when you did something well?
- Can you describe a time when your teacher thought you did a good job?
- Do you often tell your friends when they do something well?

### Food

- What is your favourite food?
- Is there any food you dislike?

### Pets and Animals

- What's your favourite animal, and why?
- Where would you prefer to keep a pet, indoors or outdoors?
- Have you ever had a pet before?
- What is the most popular animal in China?

### Sports Teams

- Have you ever been part of a sports team?
